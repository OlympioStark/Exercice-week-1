About domestics cats

There are often called house cats when kept at indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them other felids and felines. They are often valued by humans for companionship.

There are more than seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat registries..

Cat senses

  • They can hear sounds too faint or too high in frequency for humans ears
  • They can see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and a better sense of smell than humans

History

Cats were venerated in ancient Egypt, but there may have instances of domestication as early as the Neolithic from around 9,500years ago(7500 BC).

The lions

Present in Africa and India, the lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense forests. It is usually more diumal than other big cats, but when persecuted adapt to being active at night and at twilight. A lion pride consists of a few adult males, related females and cubs. Prides vary in size and composition from three to 20 adult lions, depending on habitat and prey availability. Females cooperate when hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffe.
